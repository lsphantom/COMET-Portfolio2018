Who we are

The COMET Program is part of the University Corporation for Atmospheric Research's (UCAR's) Community Programs (UCP). We've been designing effective, scientifically accurate instruction in multiple languages and formats since 1989.

What we offer

  • E-learning (including case-based lessons, simulations, and video-based instruction)
  • Webinars
  • Synchronous virtual courses
  • Residence courses
  • Instructional design services
  • Multimedia/web production services
  • Assessment

Our approach

We value scientific accuracy, measurable performance improvement, learner engagement, and learning retention. Our team of instructional designers, scientists, graphic artists, and web developers is committed to helping you refine your training objectives and produce lessons that help your learners meet them.

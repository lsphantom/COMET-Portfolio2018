SIMULATION: NOAA

Fire Weather Communications: Clear Communications

Training Objectives

Proactively communicate with forecasters, partners and stakeholders including other offices, Regional/National Centers, IMETs, and Geographic Coordination Center Meteorologists to ensure that a consistent message is created/communicated to the public.

Solution

This branching simulation is a great way for learners to see the consequences of their choices and adjust their communication. They can do this in the safety of the module rather than endangering lives while they learn how to communicate with their partners. After three tries, it offers specific feedback about things that learners need to improve.

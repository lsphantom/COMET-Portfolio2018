SIMULATION: NOAA/NWS

Predicting Convective Cessation for Aviation Forecasters

Training Objectives

Forecast transitions in the synoptic and mesoscale pre- and post-storm environments for various atmospheric scenarios; translate these transitions into a TAF; and identify the convective mode and forecast the convection's path within a TAF framework.

Solution

This case simulation asks learners to decide if and when a TAF should be amended. It features a timed release of products to mimic what forecasters experience while working. The timed aspect adds a little pressure for forecasters to work quickly and make the hard yes/no choices that are required in aviation forecasting. Occasional distractions have intentionally been included to model the interruptions learners might encounter on the forecast desk.

DRILL: U.S. NAVY

Weather Observing Fundamentals

About this project

COMET creates drills to help learners hone desired skills. This lesson, aimed at U.S. Navy personnel and civilians tasked with reporting weather observations, details standard procedures for taking accurate observations. The drill below helps learners memorize cloud types and weather symbols. It adapts as the learner progresses, showing more of what the learner has missed.

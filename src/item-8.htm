INFORMATIONAL VIDEO: NOAA

COSMIC-2: New Generation Atmospheric Sounding Capabilities

About this project

COMET produces artistic renderings of future processes and events. In 2018, the joint U.S./Taiwan radio occultation mission FORMOSAT/COSMIC-2 will launch six satellites into low-inclination orbits, which will greatly increase the number of atmospheric and ionospheric observations being made. The short video below describes this mission and includes simulation of a Falcon Heavy rocket launch and a 3D rendering of a COSMIC-2 constellation satellite in space.
